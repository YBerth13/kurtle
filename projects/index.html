  • songlink

    Share music with anyone.

    Songlink allows you to share your favorite song or your current jam with anyone. Simply search for the song or paste a Spotify or Apple Music url, and then share the songlink that is created for you. It matters not whether the recipients use Spotify, Apple Music or Youtube, they will be able to quickly and easily listen to the shared song.

  • Reedio.fm

    Groove to some tunage here.

    Reedio.fm is a web app that serves dynamic radio stations for over 1000 music genres. Powered by Youtube and the echonest, Reedio.fm provides an iteractive music discovery experience. It is built with React.js on the front end and node/express on the backend.

  • Novel Sense Reader

    Available on the Apple App Store for free download.

    Novel Sense Reader leverages Spritz speed reading technology and soothing background music to deliver a unique ebook reading experience. Colors and music are dyanmically generated based on the emotion of the words being read. Thousands of free ebooks can easily be downloaded and enjoyed.
